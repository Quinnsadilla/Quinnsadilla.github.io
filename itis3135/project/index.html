Welcome to Girls Who Code at Charlotte!

Girls Who Code or GWS is an international nonprofit organization. It aims to support women in computer science and help bring inspire young girls to start coding.

Here at UNC Chrlotte our mission is to uplift and maintain a strong community for women in computer science, as well as develop technical and professional skills. We welcome all levels of technical skills, from no-experience to experienced! Anyone is welcome to join the organization including trans and gender non-conforming allies.
